Winnie the Pooh Crochet

Welcome to Creativejourney! Our recent project showcases the crochet of carnation flowers, a perfect gift idea for Mother's Day. Easy to crochet and beautifully crafted, these flowers capture the essence of love and appreciation. Let us help you create something special for your loved ones with our heartfelt designs.

Material:
1.5mm crochet hook
Ginger yellow yarn
Red yarn
Cotton
8mm eyes
Keychain

Hand: (Yellow Yarn)
R1: 6x
R2-R4: 6x
*Change to red yarn*
R5: 6x
+ Add cotton
Break the yarn.

Head: (Yellow Yarn)
R1: 6x
R2: 6v
R3: 6(x, v)
R4: 6(2x, v)
R5: 6(3x, v)
R6: 6(4x, v)
R7-R13: 36x
R14: 6(4x, a)
R15: 6(3x, a)
R16: 6(2x, a)
R17: 6(x, a)
+ Add cotton
R18: 6a
Break the yarn.

Body: (Yellow Yarn)
R1: 6x
R2: 6v
R3: 6(x, v)
R4: 6(2x, v)
R5-R7: 24x
*Change to red yarn*
R8-R9: 24x
R10: 6(2x, a)
R11: 6(x, a)
+ Add cotton
Break the yarn.

Leg: (Yellow Yarn)
R1: 6x
R2: 6v
R3-R4: 6x
R5: 3(2x, a)
+ Add cotton
Break the yarn.

Ear: (Yellow Yarn)
R1: 6x
R2: 6v
R3-R5: 6x
Break the yarn.

Nose: (Black Yarn)
Use a needle and stitch an upside-down triangle to create the nose.
