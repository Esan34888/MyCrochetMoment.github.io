Carnation Flower Crochet

Welcome to Creativejourney! Our recent project showcases the crochet of carnation flowers, a perfect gift idea for Mother's Day. Easy to crochet and beautifully crafted, these flowers capture the essence of love and appreciation. Let us help you create something special for your loved ones with our heartfelt designs.

Crochet Diagram

Basic Crochet Stitches Diagram

Material:
1.5mm crochet hook
Green yarn
Red yarn
6mm wire
stick for the flower
Cotton

Green Yarn:
R1: 10f
R2: 10v
R3: 20f
R4: 20v
*Change to any color you like (Red Yarn)*
R5: 40v
R6: 80v
R7: (X, 3ch) until the end
Break the yarn.
Use green yarn to work around the front post double crochet stitches around the edge.
